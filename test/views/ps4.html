Medal of Honor Recipients


The Meusue Argonne Cemetary contains the graves of 9 Medal of Honor Recepients. More than any other American cemetary

Erwin Russell Bleckley - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism October 06, 1918 in the Air near Binarville, France

Marcellus Holms Chiles - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism November 03, 1918 at Le Champy Bas, France

Matej Kocak - Earned BOTH the ARMY and NAVY Medal of Honor During World War I For the SAME ACT of heroism July 18, 1918 at Soissons, France

Frank Luke, Jr. - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism September 29, 1918 in the Air near Murvaux, France

Oscar F. Miller - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism September 28, 1918 at Gesnes, France

Harold W. Roberts - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism October 04, 1918 at Montrebeau Woods, France

William Sawelson - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism October 26, 1918 at Grand-Pre, France

Fred E. Smith - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism September 29, 1918 at Binarville, France

Freddie Stowers - Earned The Medal of Honor During World War I For heroism September 28, 1918 at Hill 188, Champagne Marne Sector, France
